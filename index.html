A prominent name in the Testing, Inspection, and Certification (TIC) industry aimed to enhance brand awareness and establish a stronger presence among larger competitors. To achieve this, we conducted a comprehensive analysis of their B2B business model, focusing on identifying their potential audience. Through in-depth market research, we mapped key industry trends and pinpointed decision-makers within the target sectors. This informed our strategy to create tailored organic and paid campaigns designed to resonate with the audience. We developed compelling content that highlighted unique value propositions, ensuring it was distributed across effective channels. For the organic campaign, we enhanced online presence through LinkedIn articles, engaging social media posts, blogs and targeted email marketing efforts. This improved visibility and established credibility in the industry. Simultaneously, we launched paid advertising campaigns on platforms like LinkedIn and Google Ads, driving impressions and engagement among decision-makers. By continuously optimizing these campaigns based on real-time data and feedback, we successfully directed attention to the offerings. The result was a significant boost in brand awareness and engagement metrics, effectively positioning the brand against larger competitors in the TIC sector.
